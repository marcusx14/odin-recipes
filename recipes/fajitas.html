Fajita's

Arrachera or inside skirt is normally one of the preferred beef cuts for high end tacos. It has the right amount of fat and a great meat taste that few cuts can rival. If you want to eat it like a Mexican, try to make an "arriera" sauce to garnish - chop 5 jalapeno peppers, 1/2 onion and a bunch of cilantro. Combine all and add the juice of 2 limes and the same amount of olive oil finally add 3 bulbs garlic. Salt to taste and let marinate for as long as possible.

Ingredients

  • 1 Skirt or flank steak
  • 1 bulb of a Clove garlic
  • A bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 3 limes
  • olive oil equal to the juice of 2 limes
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Red, 1 green and one yellow bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • one package flour tortillas or corn
  • 5 Jalapenos
