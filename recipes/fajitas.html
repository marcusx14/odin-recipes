Fajita's

Arrachera or inside skirt is normally one of the preferred beef cuts for high end tacos. It has the right amount of fat and a great meat taste that few cuts can rival. If you want to eat it like a Mexican, try to make an "arriera" sauce to garnish - chop 5 jalapeno peppers, 1/2 onion and a bunch of cilantro. Combine all and add the juice of 2 limes and the same amount of olive oil finally add 3 bulbs garlic. Salt to taste and let marinate for as long as possible.

Ingredients

  • 1 skirt or flank steak
  • 1 bulb of a clove garlic
  • A bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 3 limes
  • olive oil equal to the juice of 3 limes
  • 2 onions
  • 1 red, 1 green and one yellow bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 package flour tortillas or corn
  • 5 jalapenos

    • Steps

    1. Marinate: Place Steak in to a large ziplock freezer bag.
    2. Add the juice of 3 limes with and equal portion of olive oil to the bag.
    3. Mince garlic and add to the bag.
    4. Add a tablespoon of salt.
    5. Add a bunch of cilantro leaves.
    6. Add desired amount of finely chopped jalapeno or sereno peppers.
    7. Seal the bag squeezing out as much air as possible.
    8. Knead the marinade ingredients together and into the steak. (From outside the bag of course.)
    9. Place in refridgerator for at least 30mins, preferably overnight if time allows it.
    10. Prepare: Slice bell peppers into 1/2" rings.
    11. Slice onions into 1/4" rings.
    12. Decore and slice one jalapeno pepper in half.
    13. Place Steak, onions and bell peppers on the grill.
    14. Place the jalapeno slices on the steak.
    15. Cook at 325-425 degrees, flipping once, and cooked to your preference.
    16. Also make sure the peppers and onions are grilled well.
    17. Serve with bell peppers and steamed tortillas.
