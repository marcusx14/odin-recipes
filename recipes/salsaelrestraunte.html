Salsa Restraunte

Ingredients

  • 1 can whole tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • sqeeze of lime
  • 1 jalapeno

Preparation

  1. Quarter 1 onion.
  2. Cut 1 jalapeno or sereno pepper in half and remove the seeds and core.
  3. Put all ingredients into a blender.
  4. Add fresh Sqeezed lime juice. (If you havent already.)
  5. Blend on chop setting till desired texture.
  6. Serve with fresh tortilla chips.
